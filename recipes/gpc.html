Grilled Peaches and Cream

Description

This is an easy grilled peach dessert! A wonderful way to use in season peaches, with the result being a caramalized creamy treat.

Ingredients

  • 4 peaches, halved and pitted
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 cup soft cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup of nuts of your choosing
  • 1 tsp oil

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill for medium-high heat.
  2. Brush peaches with a light coating of oil.
  3. Place pit side down onto the grill. Grill for 5 minutes, or until the surfaces have nice grill marks.
  4. Turn the peaches over, and drizzle with a bit of honey. Place a dollop of the cream cheese spread in the place where the pit was.
  5. Grill for 2 to 3 more minutes, or until the filling is warm.
  6. Place peach on a plate and sprinkle nuts.
  7. Serve immediately.
